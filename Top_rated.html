The Iliad

After ten years of siege by Greek forces, the walls of Troy remain intact. Only the intervention of the greatest warrior of all time, Achilles, can tilt the balance in favor of the invaders. On the Trojan side, Prince Hector is the only one who can face him. This is the story of the greatest war of all time.

The Odyssey

Fresh from his triumphs in the Trojan War, Odysseus, King of Ithaca, wants nothing more than to return home to his family. Instead, he offends the sea god, Poseidon, who dooms him to years of shipwreck and wandering. Battling man-eating monsters and violent storms, Odysseus will need all his strength and cunning.

Mahabharata

Veda Vyasa’s Mahabharata is a timeless tale of brotherhood, deceit, love, and sacrifice, also featuring The Gita, Lord Krishna’s discourse on dharma. This 42-comic box set brings the epic to life. Volume 1 covers the Pandavas’ early days in Hastinapur, Duryodhana’s growing hatred, and their escape from the burning palace. Volume 2 follows the fateful game of dice, leading to the Pandavas' exile. Volume 3 encapsulates the great war of Kurukshetra, ending in their victory with Krishna’s guidance.
